About Us

Developer Student Club UMiami is a university-based community group by Google Developers for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

By joining a DSC, students expand their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. Don't worry if you've never coded before, we have workshops for everyone.
